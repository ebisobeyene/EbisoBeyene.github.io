Lake Abijatta

Lake Abijatta is an alkaline lake in Ethiopia. It lies in the Main Ethiopian Rift Valley south of Addis Ababa, inside the Abijatta-Shalla National Park.

Lake Langano

Lake Langano is 18 kilometres long and 16 km wide, with a surface area of 230 square kilometres and a maximum depth of 46 metres.

Abijatta-Shalla National Park

Located in the Oromia Region, 200 km south of Addis Ababa and east of the Batu–Shashamane highway, this park is a stunning stretch of Ethiopian highlands.
